Hydrogels

Purilon Gel

This is a gentle, sterile hydrogel consisting of natural ingredients (purified water, sodium carboxymethylcellulose and calcium alginate) without any additives. It promotes wound healing by effective and gentle debridement.

The gel may be used throughout the healing process to provide a moist healing environment.

Indications: Necrotic and slough wounds (leg ulcers, pressure ulcers, non-infected diabetic foot ulcers, 1st/2nd degree burns).
Available Size: 8g

Exudate Management

Advazorb Border

This is a foam dressing with soft perforated silicon wound contact layer and adhesive border. Designed for moderate to low exudating wounds.

Offers atraumatic experience to the wound bed; its self-adhesive border dressing comfortably stays in place minimizing potential for maceration.

Available Sizes:
• 10cm x 10cm
• 10cm x 20cm
• 15cm x 15cm
